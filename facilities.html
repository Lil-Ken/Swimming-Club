Our Facilities

Experience swimming excellence with our top-tier facilities at Blue Ocean Club. From Olympic Size Pool to Sauna Room, we provide everything you need for a rewarding "Blue Ocean" journey.

Olympic Sized Swimming Pool

Our Olympic Sized Swimming Pool offers the perfect environment for swimmers of all levels, from beginners to seasoned athletes. With its spacious dimensions and crystal-clear water, our pool provides ample space for training, fitness, and competitive swimming.

Children's Swimming Pool

It's a safe and fun spot for kids to splash and play. With shallow waters and colorful surroundings, it's perfect for little ones to enjoy while building water confidence. Lifeguards are always on hand for added safety. Come join the fun!

Blue Ocean Canteen

Make a splash and satisfy your cravings at the Blue Ocean Canteen! We offer a refreshing variety of snacks and beverages to fuel your swim or refuel after a fun day at the pool. Whether you need a quick bite or a cool drink, catch up with friends or enjoy a solo treat, our canteen has something delicious for everyone.

Hot & Cold Shower Room

Step into our refreshing hot and cold shower rooms at the Swimming Club. After an invigorating swim, indulge in a revitalizing shower experience. Whether you prefer a warm embrace or a cool blast, our facilities ensure you leave feeling refreshed and rejuvenated. Wash away the day's stresses and emerge feeling renewed, ready to dive back into the pool or head out with a renewed sense of energy.

Blue Ocean Sauna Room

Unwind and soothe your body and mind in our luxurious sauna at the Swimming Club. Step into a tranquil oasis where the heat melts away tension, leaving you feeling relaxed and rejuvenated. Let the gentle warmth envelop you as you escape the hustle and bustle of the day. With its calming ambiance and health benefits, our sauna is the perfect retreat for ultimate relaxation and wellness.

Blue Ocean Gym Room

Get ready to elevate your fitness game in our state-of-the-art gym room. Whether you're a beginner or a fitness enthusiast, our gym offers a diverse range of equipment to cater to your workout needs. From cardio machines to strength training equipment, we've got everything you need to achieve your fitness goals. With a supportive environment and expert guidance, our gym is your go-to destination for a healthier, stronger you.

Blue Ocean Club

Welcome to Blue Ocean Club! We're dedicated to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment where swimmers of all ages and abilities can thrive. Whether you're just starting your swimming journey or aiming for competitive success, we have something for everyone.
